1) Current Rules Used in System

Interest (reducing-balance):
• New clients (<3 completed loans): 30% p.a.
• Returning clients (3+ completed loans): 28% p.a.

Fees:
• Initiation fee: 15% of principal on every loan
• Service fee: R60 per month
• Credit life: Removed

Core Formula

monthlyRate = annualRate / 12
PMT = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n − 1]
totalInterest = (PMT × n) − P
totalRepayment = P + totalInterest + (P×0.15) + (60×n)
